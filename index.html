Our water

We can’t take credit for our water: A long 15 year journey through the French Alps gives it naturally occurring electrolytes and minerals and that distinctive cool, crisp taste. We sustainably collect what nature gives us at the source so you can enjoy natural spring water anytime, anywhere.

Our story

We’ve been proud to share our natural spring water with the world for over 230 years, but old age doesn’t mean we’re settling down and sticking with the status quo when it comes to our products or our mission. From sustainability innovations to exciting designer collaborations, we’re always thirsty for fresh challenges and creating new solutions to benefit today and future generations.

Our sustainability actions
